# BITUNCOIN Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The BITUNCOIN community is dedicated to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants. We are committed to fostering a community built upon core values of:

- **Dignity**: Honoring the inherent worth and potential of every individual
- **Inclusion**: Creating spaces where diverse voices are heard, valued, and amplified
- **Ceremonial Respect**: Acknowledging the significance of our collaborative work and treating each interaction with intentionality and honor
- **Alignment with BITUNCOIN Hybrid Sovereign License v1.0**: Upholding the principles of sovereignty, transparency, and equitable benefit-sharing that define our movement

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

Contributors to BITUNCOIN projects are expected to:

- Treat all community members with respect, dignity, and compassion
- Listen actively to diverse perspectives and engage constructively with differing viewpoints
- Give credit and acknowledge the contributions of others
- Focus on what is best for the community and its mission
- Show empathy toward other community members
- Respect the intellectual property rights and sovereignty of contributors
- Demonstrate commitment to the values of the BITUNCOIN movement
- Conduct themselves with integrity in all community spaces, both digital and physical

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse of any kind based on identity characteristics including but not limited to: race, color, ethnicity, national origin, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, religion, disability, or any other protected status
- Derogatory language, slurs, or intentional misgendering
- Intimidation, threats, or coercion
- Unwelcome sexual or romantic advances
- Public or private harassment
- Sharing others' private information without consent (doxxing)
- Trolling, spam, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors
- Abuse of power or authority to silence, marginalize, or undermine community members
- Intellectual property theft or violation of the BITUNCOIN Hybrid Sovereign License v1.0

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All BITUNCOIN project repositories and codebases
- Community forums, discussion boards, and chat channels
- In-person events, conferences, and gatherings associated with BITUNCOIN
- Official social media accounts and communications
- Any space where community members identify themselves as part of the BITUNCOIN project

The Code of Conduct applies both to spaces managed by the BITUNCOIN community and to the extent permitted by law in public spaces where individuals represent the BITUNCOIN community.

## Enforcement

Community members who observe violations of this Code of Conduct are encouraged to report them. Reports can be made confidentially to the BITUNCOIN community leadership.

### Reporting Process

1. **Report Details**: Provide specific information about the incident, including dates, times, locations, and the individuals involved
2. **Confidentiality**: Reports will be handled with appropriate confidentiality to protect the safety and privacy of all parties
3. **Investigation**: The community leadership will conduct a fair, timely, and thorough investigation
4. **Response**: Appropriate action will be taken based on the investigation findings

### Consequences

Individuals who violate this Code of Conduct may face consequences including, but not limited to:

- A private warning and request to cease the behavior
- Temporary suspension from community spaces
- Permanent removal from the community and project repositories
- Other remedial actions as deemed appropriate by community leadership

The severity of consequences will be proportional to the severity and context of the violation.

## Appeal Process

Individuals who believe they have been unfairly sanctioned under this Code of Conduct may request an appeal to a review panel for consideration of their case.

## Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant (version 2.1, available at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), adapted and extended to reflect the unique values and principles of the BITUNCOIN community and the BITUNCOIN Hybrid Sovereign License v1.0.

## License Alignment

All participants in the BITUNCOIN community agree to conduct themselves in accordance with both this Code of Conduct and the terms of the BITUNCOIN Hybrid Sovereign License v1.0. In the event of any conflict, the values of dignity, inclusion, and ceremonial respect shall take precedence in interpretation and enforcement.

## Contact

For questions about this Code of Conduct, concerns about community conduct, or to report violations, please contact the BITUNCOIN community leadership through official channels.
